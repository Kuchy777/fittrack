FitTrack is a lightweight health diary for people who want to stay in shape without complications.

Features:
• Meal diary – add a product from the database or snap a photo of your meal with the camera, log calories and macros
• Automatic daily kcal goal – Mifflin–St Jeor formula based on weight, height, activity and goal (maintain, lose, gain)
• Workouts – simple activity logging (type, duration, kcal burned)
• Low-calorie recipes – library of simple, light meals
• Local reminders – water every 2 h, meals every 4 h, movement once a day. No server, no Firebase.
• Full privacy – account on your own backend, data does not go to third parties

FitTrack does not collect marketing data, does not show ads and does not require location.

The app requires an account on the FitTrack backend (defaults to http://10.0.2.2:8080 for the emulator — your own server in production).

Requirements:
• Android 8.0 (API 26) or newer
• Camera access (optional, for meal photos)
• Notifications (Android 13+ requires permission)

Open-source project: https://github.com/Kuchy777/fittrack
